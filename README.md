# App to remind me to check on my kids 

## Overview
A simple automated timer system that sends regular reminders to check on children at configurable intervals. The product focuses on helping parents maintain awareness of their kids' activities and safety through consistent prompting.

## Problem
Parents can get absorbed in work, household tasks, or other activities and lose track of time, potentially missing important moments or safety checks with their children

## Target User
Parents and caregivers who want regular reminders to check on their children during busy periods

## Features
- Configurable timer intervals (1-60 minutes)
- Multiple reminder notification types (sound, visual, mobile push)
- Pause/resume timer functionality
- Simple start/stop controls
- Basic customizable reminder messages
